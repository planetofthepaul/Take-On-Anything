About

What this is and why it exists.

Take On Anything is a collection of free online tools for developers, designers, and anyone who writes code.

Every tool on this site follows three rules:

  • No ads. Not now, not ever. No pop-ups, no banners, no sponsored placements.
  • No login. No account creation, no email harvesting, no tracking cookies.
  • No bloat. Each tool does one thing well, loads fast, and stays out of your way.

Most online dev tools started simple and useful, then got buried under ads, account walls, and feature creep. This site exists because those tools should stay simple and free.

How it works

Every tool runs entirely in your browser. Your code never touches a server. Nothing is stored or transmitted anywhere. The site is hosted on GitHub Pages — no servers to maintain, no data to leak.

Analytics

This site uses Plausible Analytics, a privacy-friendly, cookie-free analytics tool. No personal data is collected. No cookies are set. You can read more about how Plausible works on their data policy page.

Who built this
