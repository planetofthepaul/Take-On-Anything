Frequently Asked Questions

Do flag emojis work on all devices?

Most flag emojis work on iOS, Android, Mac, and modern browsers. However, Windows has limited flag emoji support — flags may appear as two-letter country codes instead of flag images on some Windows devices.

How many country flag emojis are there?

There are over 250 flag emojis in Unicode, covering virtually every country and territory, plus special flags like the rainbow flag 🏳️‍🌈 and pirate flag 🏴‍☠️.
