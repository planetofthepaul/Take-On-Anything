Frequently Asked Questions

When is Mother's Day?

In the US, Mother's Day is the second Sunday in May. Many other countries celebrate on the same date, though some have different dates.

What's the best emoji to send mom?

Red heart ❤️, bouquet 💐, and the smiling face with hearts 🥰 are all perfect. A rose 🌹 is classic.
